Current State

- Infrastructure under this bill to be federally owned, state operated.
- Eminent domain to be used for repaired/replaced infrastructure.
- Provide conditional grants to private companies to create parallel grids or upgrade existent infrastructure, to be conditioned on employing citizens from affected areas of Pacifica and for priority usage privileges for the federal government during emergencies.
- Create a FEMA program to provide emergency generators to the Pacifica grid and point-to-point directional antennas for residential Pacifica areas in order to facilitate WiFi access.

# Draft of the Restoration Act of 2025

The following provisions were included:
- Reconstruction, repairs and new construction of energy infrastructure in the District of Pacifica
- Use of eminent domain under 28 U.S.C. §1403 with compensation stipulated in 42 U.S.C. §4651
- Grant programs for construction of parallel girds, conditional upon >60% employment from the affected ZIP-codes and others
- Emergency generators will be provided by FEMA to what I approximate to be (following current appropriation levels)
- 100 hospitals
- 250 substations or utility nodes
- 200 water/wastewater treatment facilities
- 500 shelters or community resilience centres
- Residential Connectivity Program to provide internet access to what I approximate to be 25,000 households by current appropriation levels
- Enhanced periodical appropriation for cybersecurity in energy grids purposes
