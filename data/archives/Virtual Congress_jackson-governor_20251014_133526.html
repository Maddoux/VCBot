EXECUTIVE ORDER 1
Assesment of our State's cybersecurity.

In August 10th the State of Jackson, among others, suffered a cyber attack. It was contained, but it has shown the need to be prepared for such occurrences. This is why, as Governor, I hereby order the following:

-For all State agencies to carry out an audit of their cybersecurity infrastructure and capabilities.

-For the creation of the Cybersecurity Assesment Commission (to be staffed and managed by cybersecurity experts and law enforcement officers designated by the Jackson Department of Public Safety), that will have the purpose of compiling said audits, carry out a digital forensic investigation into the origin of the attack, and create a report detailing the state of Jackson's cybersecurity infrastructure.

-For said report to be shared, along with my own office, with the Department of Homeland Security.

-For all of the actions in this Order to be carried out in less than 45 days, unless otherwise extended.

It is so ordered by the power vested in me as Governor of the State of Jackson.

Signed:
Greg Stillson
